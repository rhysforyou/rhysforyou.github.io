Parked for iPhone

Parked is an iPhone app designed to help you remember where you parked and remind you before your parking ticket runs out. It has a few, select key features:

  • Super fast—Designed with speed in mind, Parked makes it quick and easy to set up your parking details so you can get on with your life.
  • Remember where you parked—Parked grabs your location as soon as you park and can give you walking directions back to your car.
  • Stay alert—When your parking is about to run out, Parked will give you a friendly reminder. You can also see how long you've got left at a glance.
  • Make a note of it—Need to remember what level of the parking garage you were on? Parked gives you a free-form notes field where you can write any useful information you need to remember.

Parked aims to give you one less thing to think about when you're going out. The less time you're looking at our app, the happier we are.
